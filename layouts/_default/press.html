Press & Media Resources

Information and resources for journalists covering accountability issues in St. Clair County.

For Journalists

We welcome inquiries from journalists investigating issues of public accountability in St. Clair County. This site is designed to serve as a resource for reporters who need quick access to court documents, depositions, and other primary sources.

What We Offer

  • Access to organized court documents
  • Searchable deposition transcripts
  • Chronological event timelines
  • Cross-referenced people and incidents
  • Citation-ready document references

Source Verification

  • All documents from official sources
  • FOIA request numbers tracked
  • Court filing references included
  • Page numbers cited for quotes
  • Original PDFs available for download

Attribution Guidelines

When citing documents from this site, we recommend the following approaches:

For Primary Documents:

Cite the original source (e.g., "according to the deposition of [Name] in [Case Name]"). Our site can be referenced as where you accessed the document.

For Our Analysis:

"St. Clair Under Oath, a public accountability website, reported that..." with a link to the specific page.

Each Page Has Citation Tools:

Look for the "Cite This" button on document pages for properly formatted citations in APA, MLA, and Chicago styles.

Media Inquiries

For press inquiries, interview requests, or to verify information, journalists may contact us. Please include your name, outlet, and deadline.
