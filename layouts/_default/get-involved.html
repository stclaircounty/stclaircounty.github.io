File FOIA Requests

Public records belong to the public. Use Michigan's Freedom of Information Act to obtain documents.

  • Request incident reports
  • Ask for communications
  • Obtain policy documents

Vote

The Sheriff is an elected position. Make your voice heard at the ballot box.

  • Register to vote
  • Research candidates
  • Vote in every election

Have Information to Share?

If you have documents, firsthand accounts, or other evidence related to misconduct at the St. Clair County Sheriff's Office, there are safe ways to share that information.

Important Considerations:

  • Only share documents you have legal right to share
  • Consider consulting with an attorney if you're unsure about whistleblower protections
  • Be aware that email and standard communication may not be private

For sensitive information, consider contacting investigative journalists at reputable news organizations who have secure methods for receiving tips.
