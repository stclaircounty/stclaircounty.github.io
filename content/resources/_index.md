## Understanding Your Rights

### Freedom of Information Act (FOIA)

Michigan's FOIA law gives you the right to access public records. Here's how to use it:

1. **Submit a written request** to the agency holding the records
2. **Be specific** about what documents you're requesting
3. **Agencies must respond** within 5 business days
4. **Fees may apply** for copies and search time
5. **Appeal denials** to the agency head, then to circuit court

### Where to Send FOIA Requests

- **St. Clair County Sheriff's Office** — law enforcement records
- **St. Clair County Clerk's Office** — county government records
- **31st Circuit Court** — court records
- **Individual municipalities** — local government records
