## Deposition Overview

This deposition contains sworn testimony from Sheriff Mat King regarding the November 6, 2022 OWI arrest of his brother, Deputy Marc King, and the subsequent handling of the matter by the St. Clair County Sheriff's Office.

## Key Issues Addressed

### Conflict of Interest

Sheriff King was questioned about his decision not to recuse himself from a matter involving his own brother:

- He acknowledged the family relationship
- He was asked about department policies on recusal
- He was questioned about his direct involvement post-arrest

### Command Involvement

The deposition addresses Sheriff King's actions immediately following the arrest:

- Notification of the arrest
- Communications with Captain Pohl
- Communications with the Undersheriff
- Decisions about how to handle the matter internally

### Investigation Handling

Key questions about the investigation:

- Why no external agency was engaged
- What internal procedures were followed
- Who was assigned to handle the matter
- What documentation was created

## Significance

This deposition is critical because it provides sworn testimony from the highest-ranking official in the department about:

1. His knowledge of the conflict of interest
2. His decision-making process
3. The department's handling of officer misconduct
4. The absence of recusal policies
