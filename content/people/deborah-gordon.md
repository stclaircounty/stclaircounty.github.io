## Role

Deborah L. Gordon is a Michigan attorney whose practice includes civil rights and employment litigation. She appears in public court dockets representing plaintiffs in federal civil cases related to St. Clair County matters.

## Professional Background

Attorney Gordon operates Deborah L. Gordon, PLC, and has experience in employment discrimination, whistleblower protection, and civil rights law.

## Role in Related Litigation

Attorney Gordon serves as plaintiff's counsel in federal litigation involving St. Clair County Sheriff's Office personnel.
