## Role in Key Incidents

Deputy Goodrich is a deputy with the St. Clair County Sheriff's Office.

## November 2022 OWI Incident

Deputy Goodrich's involvement in the November 6, 2022 OWI arrest incident is documented through deposition testimony:

- Received a text message from Captain Matthew Pohl at 5:44 PM on November 6, 2022
- The text informed him of Deputy Marc King's OWI arrest
- This communication demonstrates how quickly information about the arrest spread through informal department channels

## Significance

The text message from Captain Pohl to Deputy Goodrich is significant because:

1. **Timing**: Sent at 5:44 PM on the day of the arrest
2. **Informal Channels**: Information spread through personal contacts rather than official reporting
3. **Pattern of Conduct**: Part of broader pattern of command staff informing friends about the arrest
4. **Evidentiary Value**: Referenced in Captain Pohl's deposition (Page 200) as evidence of communications

This information comes from Captain Matthew Pohl's deposition testimony regarding his communications on November 6, 2022.
