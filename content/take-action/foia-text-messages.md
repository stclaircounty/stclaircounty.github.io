## FOIA Request Template — Text Messages

Use this template to request text message records from the St. Clair County Sheriff's Office.

---

### Ready-to-Send Request

**To:** St. Clair County Sheriff's Office FOIA Coordinator

**Subject:** Freedom of Information Act Request — Text Message Records

---

**Request:**

Pursuant to the Michigan Freedom of Information Act (MCL 15.231 et seq.), I am requesting copies of the following records:

1. **All text messages, SMS messages, and messaging app communications** (including but not limited to iMessage, WhatsApp, Signal, and similar platforms) sent or received by the following individuals related to the November 6, 2022 OWI arrest of Deputy Marc King:

   - Sheriff Mat King
   - Captain Matthew Pohl
   - Deputy Duva
   - Undersheriff (name)
   - Any other command staff involved

2. **Time period:** November 1, 2022 through December 31, 2022

3. **Devices:** Including both work-issued devices AND personal devices used for public business

**Statutory Basis:**

Under Michigan law, text messages discussing public business are public records regardless of whether they are stored on personal or work devices. *See Bisio v. City of Village of Clarkston*, Michigan Court of Appeals (2012).

**Format Requested:**

Electronic format (PDF or native format) preferred.

**Fee Waiver Request:**

I request a waiver of fees as this information is in the public interest and concerns government accountability.

### Tips

- Keep a copy of your request
- Note the date you submitted it
- If denied, you have the right to appeal
- Excessive fee quotes can be challenged

---

### Why This Matters

Multiple officers have admitted in sworn testimony that text messages from this period were deleted. Your FOIA request:

- Creates an official record of what exists (or doesn't)
- Documents the agency's response
- Supports accountability efforts
- May reveal additional information
